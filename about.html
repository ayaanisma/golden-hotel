GOLDEN hotel established

Golden Hotel was established in 2023 with the aim of providing high-quality hospitality and comfortable accommodation for travelers and visitors. Since its establishment, the hotel has been committed to delivering excellent service and ensuring customer satisfaction. The hotel is strategically located in Mogadishu, Somalia, near Aden Adde International Airport, making it a convenient choice for both local and international guests. Its prime location allows easy access to major transportation routes and key areas of the city. With its modern facilities, professional staff, and peaceful environment, Golden Hotel continues to serve as a reliable destination for business travelers, tourists, and families seeking comfort and convenience in Mogadishu.
